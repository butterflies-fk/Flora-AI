Plant Tips

Care guides & pest prevention for healthy plants

Plant Care
Essential habits to keep your plants thriving
Care

Check Before You Water

Stick your finger an inch into the soil. If it still feels damp, wait a day or two. Overwatering is the most common way plants die.

Care

Find the Right Spot

Most plants love bright, indirect light near a window. If your plant is leaning toward the light or losing colour, try moving it somewhere brighter.

Care

Remove Dead Leaves

Snip off any yellow or brown leaves as soon as you notice them. It keeps your plant looking healthy and stops it wasting energy on leaves it can't save.

Care

Repot When It's Cramped

If roots are poking out of the bottom of the pot, your plant needs more room. Move it to a slightly bigger pot with fresh soil — it'll thank you!

Pest & Disease Prevention
Simple steps to catch problems before they spread
Pest

Check Your Plant Weekly

Take a quick look under the leaves every week. Tiny bugs or white spots are a sign of pests. The sooner you catch them, the easier they are to deal with.

Pest

Rinse Bugs Off

Spotted bugs on your plant? A good rinse in the shower or with a hose works surprisingly well. Repeat every few days until they're gone.

Pest

Isolate New Plants

Keep any new plant away from your others for about two weeks. New plants can carry hidden pests that spread quickly to the rest of your collection.

Pest

Watch for Yellow Leaves + Wet Soil

If your plant has yellow leaves and the soil is always wet, it likely has root rot. Let it dry out, remove the soggy roots, and repot in fresh soil.
